Sign in with Discogs

You'll need a Discogs account & token to use this app.
Don't worry — it's free!

Here's how:
  1. Head to Account → Developers
  2. Click Generate token
  3. Copy the token, and enter it below

Make sure you also have a camera connected!

Your token is only ever sent to the Discogs API
